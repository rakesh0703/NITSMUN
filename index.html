NITS MUN or the Model United Nations interpretation of the National Institute of Technology, Silchar is an annual event hosted by the institute and is essentially an amalgamation of a conference and a role play, during which students imitate the work of UN organs. The story of NITS MUN traces the journey of the students of the institute who wanted to transcend the boundaries of conservative debate and look for that "beyond", who wanted to apprehend and appreciate the subtle skill that is "diplomacy", and who wanted to discover the impact that they can make in the society at large, and fundamentally represents their collective idiosyncrasies. NITSMUN is also gratified to be officially associated with United Nations Information Center for India and Bhutan(UNIC) since last two years.

ABOUT UNHRC


The United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC) is a United Nations System inter-governmental body responsible for strengthening the promotion and protection of human rights around the world. Its 47 seats are filled by member states elected for three-year terms. The General Assembly established the UNHRC by adopting a resolution (A/RES/60/251) on 15 March 2006, in order to replace the previous CHR, which had been heavily criticised for allowing countries with poor human rights records to be members. However, many of the current 47 UNHRC member states are criticised for poor human rights.The UNHRC addresses human rights-related situations in all UN member states. The UNHRC also addresses important thematic human rights issues such as freedom of association and assembly, freedom of expression, freedom of belief and religion, women's rights, LGBT rights, and the rights of racial and ethnic minorities.

ABOUT ECOFIN


The Economic and Financial Affairs Council, commonly known as the Ecofin Council, is responsible for economic policy, taxation matters, financial markets and capital movements, and economic relations with countries outside the EU. It also prepares the EU's annual budget and takes care of the legal and practical aspects of the single currency, the euro.The Ecofin Council coordinates member states' economic policies, furthers the convergence of their economic performance and monitors their budgetary policies.It also coordinates EU positions for international meetings, such as the G20, the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank. It is also responsible for the financial aspects of international negotiations on measures to tackle climate change.
